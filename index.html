FAQs

What is the return policy at Jia Personalized Gifts?

At Jia Personalized Gifts, we have a no return policy due to the nature of our products being personalized. If you need any changes or adjustments, please contact us directly.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Orders typically take 3-5 business days to process and personalize. Shipping time varies based on your location, but you can expect delivery within 7-14 business days after your order is processed.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number through the platform where you put your ORDER. You can use this number to track your package online.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Bank Transfer, G-Cash, and other secure payment options available at checkout.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email at jiapersonalizedgifts2022@gmail.com or through our Messenger. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for an additional fee. You can select this option during the checkout process.

Are there any discounts available for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please contact us directly with your order details, and we will provide you with a customized quote.

Can I customize a product not listed on your website?

If you have a specific product in mind that is not listed on our website, please reach out to us with your request. We will do our best to accommodate your needs.

How do I care for my personalized gifts?

While specific care instructions vary depending on the product, we recommend handling all personalized items with care. Some products, such as mugs, may be fragile, so please handle them gently to avoid damage.
